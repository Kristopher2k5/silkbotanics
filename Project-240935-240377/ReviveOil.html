Revive Oil

10ml $7

Thicker, fuller, and visibly healthier lashes and brows!

Elevate your beauty routine with our nourishing lash and brow serum.

  • Promotes growth and thickness of lashes and brows
  • Nourishes hair follicles, making them stronger and reducing breakage
  • Improves the appearance of lashes and brows, making them look fuller and more vibrant
  • Soothes the skin and helps nourish thinning or sparse hair

How to Use

  • Make sure your eyes are makeup-free
  • Dip the brush and apply a thin line of serum to your upper lash line and brows
  • Use Daily, best at night
  • Once you achieve your desired lash length and fullness, continue using it 2-3 times a week to maintain results

Tips

  • Be patient; results usually appear within 4–6 weeks with regular use
  • Avoid rubbing your eyes too much to protect your lashes
  • Keep the serum in a cool, dry place to preserve its potency
